5Confidence-Interval anomaly view for this item

In plain words The chart shows the change in Gross Sales (DifferenceGrossSales) for each date of this item. The two dashed lines are the global CI band computed across all items at a —% confidence level. Any point that falls outside the band is coloured red and counts as a CI anomaly. Points inside the band are shown in blue.
How to read the chart
  • Blue dots — normal changes, inside the confidence band.
  • Red dots — anomalies flagged by the CI detector: the Gross Sales change was unusually large or unusually negative compared with the rest of the dataset.
  • Dashed orange line (upper) and dashed green line (lower) — the CI boundaries. Any point above the orange or below the green line is flagged.
  • Grey dotted line at zero — reference: no change between snapshots.
